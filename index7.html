Bacos

Carne Asada Bacos: Grilled steak bacos marinated in a flavorful blend of spices and citrus, served with onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.

Vegetarian Bacos: A delicious option for vegetarians, featuring sautéed or grilled vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, and zucchini) topped with salsa, guacamole, and cheese.

Al Pastor Bacos: Thinly sliced pork marinated in achiote and pineapple, cooked on a vertical spit, and served with onions, cilantro, and pineapple chunks.

Turritos

Chicken Fajita Turrito: A flavorful turrito with grilled chicken, sautéed bell peppers and onions, rice, beans, cheese, and sour cream.

Classic Bean and Cheese Turrito: A simple yet satisfying turrito filled with refried beans, melted cheese, and optionally, rice.

Chimichanga Turrito: A deep-fried turrito filled with your choice of meat or beans, along with rice, cheese, and salsa.

Tamalos

Traditional Pork Tamalos: Tender pork simmered in a flavorful red chili sauce, wrapped in masa dough, and steamed to perfection.

Cheese and Jalapeño Tamalos: A spicy and cheesy option, with diced jalapeños and melted cheese mixed into the masa.

Chicken Tamalos: Shredded chicken mixed with a savory green or red sauce, wrapped in masa and steamed.

Quesodillos

Spinach and Mushroom Quesodillo: Sautéed spinach and mushrooms with cheese inside a grilled tortilla, for a flavorful vegetarian option

Pulled Pork Quesodillo: Tender pulled pork, cheese, and optionally, BBQ sauce or coleslaw, inside a grilled tortilla.

BBQ Chicken Quesodillo: Grilled chicken tossed in BBQ sauce, cheese, and optionally, caramelized onions, inside a grilled tortilla.

Sweets, Sides, and Drinks

Apple and Brie Quesodillo: Sliced apples, brie cheese, and optionally, caramelized onions or walnuts, inside a grilled tortilla for a sweet and savory option.

Sweet Tamalos: Tamalos filled with sweet ingredients like pineapple, raisins, cinnamon, and sugar, for a delicious dessert option.

Churros: Fried dough pastries dusted with cinnamon sugar, served with chocolate sauce or dulce de leche for dipping.

Flan: A creamy custard dessert with a caramel topping, similar to crème caramel.

Mini-Nachos: A small side of chips that you can dip in cheese or salsa as you want. Available in a large as well.

Drinks: We have a variety of bottled Pepsi and Coke products for you to choose from.
