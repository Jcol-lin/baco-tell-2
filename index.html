Baco Tell

Welcome to Baco Tell! We pride ourselves on bringing the vibrant and authentic flavors of Mexico to your palate, right from our food truck to your table. Nestled in the heart of the community, we serve up a tantalizing array of Mexican-inspired dishes, with a particular focus on the beloved taco. Each taco is a culinary masterpiece, meticulously crafted with the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations. From succulent quesadillas to tamales, our tacos are bursting with flavor and served with a side of Mexican hospitality. But our offerings don't stop there; we also offer a diverse selection of Mexican street food favorites, including quesadillas, burritos, and tamales, all prepared with the same dedication to authenticity and quality. At Baco Tell, we believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's a celebration of culture, heritage, and community. That's why we're committed to not only satisfying your hunger but also transporting you to the bustling streets of Mexico City with every bite. So, whether you're craving a quick lunch on the go or planning a fiesta with friends, join us at our food truck and let us take you on a culinary journey through the rich and flavorful traditions of Mexico.
